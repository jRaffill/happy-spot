Happy Spot is an interactive map where people can suggest and find places that make them and others happy. Our goal is to encourage people to step out of their comfort zone and explore new places near them through the eyes of their community. It is easy to forget how many cool spots are hidden everywhere, so we have compiled a way to find peoples' happy spots hidden in plain sight.

Our motivation for this project was the fact that it is sometimes hard to find new things that bring us joy. Not only does Happy Spot introduce us to the unknown, but it also allows the user to submit new locations, further contributing to our database of Happy Spots! :)
